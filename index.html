Homemade Flavored Icecream

Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients: heavy cream, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, pinch of salt, optional extras (chocolate chips, cookies, fruit).

Strawberry Ice Cream

Ingredients: heavy cream, milk, sugar, fresh strawberries, pinch of salt, optional extras (chocolate chunks, berries).

Choco-Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients: heavy cream, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, cocoa powder, pinch of salt, optional extras (chocolate chips, nuts).
