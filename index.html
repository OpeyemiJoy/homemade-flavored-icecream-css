Our Signature Flavors

Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients: heavy cream, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, pinch of salt, optional extras (chocolate chips, cookies, fruit).

Strawberry Ice Cream

Ingredients: heavy cream, milk, sugar, fresh strawberries, pinch of salt, optional extras (chocolate chunks, berries).

Choco-Vanilla Ice Cream

Ingredients: heavy cream, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, cocoa powder, pinch of salt, optional extras (chocolate chips, nuts).

Step-by-Step Instructions

Same method for all flavors

1

Mix Base Ingredients

Whisk sugar, milk, and salt together in a large bowl until fully dissolved.

2

Add Cream & Flavor

Stir in heavy cream and vanilla extract until the mixture is smooth and well combined.

3

Prepare for Freezing

Pour the mixture into a freezer-safe dish or ice cream maker container.

4

Initial Freeze

Freeze for 2 hours, then whisk vigorously to break up ice crystals.

5

Repeat Whisking

Repeat the whisking process every hour for the best creamy texture.

6

Add Mix-ins

Add chocolate chips, nuts, or fruit during the final stir before complete freezing.

7

Final Freeze & Serve

Freeze until firm (about 4-6 hours) and enjoy your homemade ice cream!
